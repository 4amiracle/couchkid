mission.

couch kid exists to help people feel like themselves. we believe emotional vulnerability shouldn't be rare or radical — it should be accessible. through street interviews, conversations, and creative expression, we let the world be emotional one feeling at a time. we meet people where they are, on the sidewalk, on the couch, and ask them to be honest. not polished. not performing. just human. couch kid is not a brand that talks at you. it's a conversation that sits with you.
